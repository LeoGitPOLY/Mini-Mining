There was a legend about a little village, far away from everything. A legend that no one could ever prove true. Indeed, it was hidden in the depth of the ground.  Some say that the person who would find this secret place, would live an unforgettable experience, and discover what they always looked for. So that’s why, one day, Mini Dig left his hometown to start this wonderful adventure.
After many exhausting travelling days, Mini Dig found the village he was looking for. He met some people from the village who were excited to help him on his new quest…
